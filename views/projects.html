Semantle Solver

Used a combination of linear algebra, Word2Vec, and natural language processing to develop a novel algorithm in order to auto-solve the Semantle word game in 3 or less guesses. Packaged in a chrome extension that also solves Worlde, Connections and Letterboxd.
